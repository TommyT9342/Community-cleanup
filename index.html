Why We're Doing This

Small actions,
real change.

A clean community isn't just about looks. It affects how safe people feel, how proud they are of where they live, and even their health.

🌱

It's Our Home

West Bedford is where we grew up, go to school, and spend our time. Keeping it clean is a way of saying we care about the place we call home.

🤝

It Builds Community

When people come together to clean their neighbourhood, it builds trust and pride. Neighbours start looking out for each other.

🌍

It Protects the Environment

Litter and waste don't just stay where they land — they get into waterways, harm wildlife, and pollute the air. Cleaning up prevents that chain reaction.
